History

Fornite was revealed at the 2011 Spike Video Game Awards. It wasn't as popular as how it was until the initial release date on July 25, 2017 that released the "Battle Royale" (BR) game mode. Enticing many players across the world which gained many players as time passed by. The only game mode released at launch was save the world which is a Players Versus Experience Base Building(PVEBB) and killing zombies. Later on in 2017 Battle Royale was released.
